Become a Friend of FixNix(FoFN)

FixNix has a robust partner program,Friend of FixNix(FoFN) which includes information, technology, and consulting partners. Partners are an important component of FixNix’s business model and solution offerings.

Information partners expand the depth Fixnix’s knowledge library and the variety of available Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) solutions. Technology partners enhanceFixNix’s integrated GRC offering.

Consulting partners leverage FixNix internally as well as to provide services to a vast array enterprises all over the world, sometimes as a FixNix reseller.


Goodies of FoFN
1.Commission payable is calculated at the rate of 10% of the value of Information Security GRC Solutions invoiced by FixNix to the Customers
2.Commission shall only be payable to the Referral Partner when the Charges for the Information Security GRC Solutions have been received by FixNix from the Customer.
3.The Referral Partner must invoice FixNix for the Commission as per the Quarterly Commission Report
4.FixNix will make payments to the Referral Partner by Bank Transfer (NEFT/RTGS) or Cheque
5.For Customers with more than 250 (two hundred and fifty) users or for other special cases involving for example price reductions or special conditions, FixNix reserves the right to negotiate a reduced Commission on a case- by-case basis.
6.Commission is paid for twenty four months from the point of first sale relating to the referral. At the end of this twenty four month period all commission payments will cease.
